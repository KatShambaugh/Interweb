Interweb

An exploratory game inspired by early 2000s personal websites.

Created by Katherine Shambaugh, 2021

Interweb is a new kind of game, a digital art time capsule of the early 2000s that tells a serious story.

Explore the personal website of 18 year old Katie Ventura, interacting with her pictures, links, and more, to unravel the mystery of her sudden disappearance.
